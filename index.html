About In-Xitu

In-Xitu is a student-driven entrepreneurial initiative dedicated to promoting biotechnology innovation, plant tissue culture awareness, scientific learning and entrepreneurial development. Through practical workshops, research activities and collaborative projects, we empower students to explore biotechnology beyond the classroom.
